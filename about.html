About Me

Hi, I'm Clare

I'm Clare Kiernan, an accredited counsellor with over 25 years' experience supporting individuals, families and couples through difficult and often overwhelming times in their lives.

I understand that reaching out for therapy isn't always easy. You might not have the right words yet — only a sense that something doesn't feel right, or that things have become difficult to manage on your own. That's okay.

How I work

I offer a warm, calm and non-judgemental space where you can begin to talk openly and feel genuinely heard. My approach is flexible and tailored to you. This means:

  • We work at a pace that feels comfortable
  • You're not expected to have everything figured out
  • We focus on what feels most important to you

Whether you're feeling anxious, overwhelmed, emotionally stuck, or struggling in your relationships, we can begin to make sense of things together.

What I can help with

Support for a range of difficulties

I support people with a range of difficulties, including:

Anxiety & overthinking

Finding calmer, steadier ground when your mind feels busy or overwhelmed.

Relationship challenges & emotional disconnection

Making sense of distance and difficulty, and rebuilding understanding and closeness.

Loss of confidence or feeling stuck

Rediscovering self-belief and a sense of direction.

Stress, overwhelm or life changes

Support through demanding periods and transitions in life.

I also work with families and couples who feel distant from one another and want to rebuild communication, understanding, and closeness.

My approach to therapy

Safe, supported and understood

I draw from a range of therapeutic approaches, but what matters most is creating a space where you feel safe, supported, and understood.

Therapy with me is not about judgement or pressure — it's about helping you explore your thoughts and feelings in a way that brings clarity, relief, and change over time.
