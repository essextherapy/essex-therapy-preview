What to expect

Your first session

Our first session is an opportunity to talk about what's bringing you here and what you would like support with. It's also a chance for you to get a feel for how I work and whether it feels right for you.

There's no pressure or obligation to continue — it's important that you feel comfortable.

Ongoing sessions

If you decide to continue, we will meet regularly and work at a pace that feels right for you. Therapy can offer:

  • A space to talk openly and feel heard
  • Support in understanding thoughts and emotions
  • A way to work through challenges and feel more grounded and in control

Cancellations

Rearranging a session

If you need to cancel or rearrange a session, I kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice.
